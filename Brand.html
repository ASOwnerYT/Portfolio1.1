Project Overview

Problem: The UX Research & Design field is growing steadily and employers are looking for candidates with more than the bare minimum Bootcamp experience.

Proposed Solution: Create an artistic and simplistic brand, code my portfolio myself, and stand out from the rest of the candidate pool.

In the beginning...

I've always been driven

  • Graduated high school with Associate's degree
  • Academic scholarship recipient
  • Graduated college as a new mom
  • Achieved dream of becoming a teacher as a single mom

After a series of life events, I realized I wanted more for myself. In the end, (which is really just the beginning) I was led to the field of UX Research & Design.
