The Wright Brand

Custom Creation

Project Overview

Problem: The UX Research & Design field is growing steadily and employers are looking for candidates with more than the bare minimum Bootcamp experience.

Proposed Solution: Create an artistic and simplistic brand, code most of my portfolio myself, and stand out from the rest of the candidate pool.

Empathy

I researched many other design portfolios to get a sense of what was trending. I love the whimsical stop-motion animation of Mortiz's Osterlau's profile picture in his portfolio and tried to bring that whimsy into my own portfolio with my lego minifigure image.

I am a proponent of minimalism much like Victoria Kazakova Simplistic is the best option when you are unfamiliar with the the product.

Ideate

Codepen has been a great resource for interaction ideas such as the growing line hover effect in the navigation bar and the timeline layout on the Wolfnest Redesign page

As a born storyteller and bibliophile, I've always loved how my initials are reflections of each other. It was important to me to include that in my Monogram design.

Test

As I continued to design, I noticed many inconsistencies and bugs. When I became too frustrated due to my novice experience, I hired a few developers on fiverr to help me complete my portfolio.

Soft-Skills Learned

  • Communicate questions, needs, and expectations
  • There's more than one way to do something
  • Don't become too attached to designs
  • Creatives think differently: embrace differences

    • Front-End Coding & Design

  • I pay more attention to creating a uniform look in regards to padding and margins.
  • Using the correct picture size is important
  • The sky is the limit! If I can think it, I can design it!
  • I still have so much to learn!
